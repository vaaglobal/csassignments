Pro Tips

How to Nail These Projects

A few things that separate good submissions from great ones.

üí¨

Use Real Empathy

Don't just say "We apologize." Actually acknowledge the customer's frustration and show you understand what they're feeling. Make it human.

‚úÇÔ∏è

Be Clear, Not Wordy

Every sentence should add value. Cut corporate jargon. Use simple language. If you can say it in 5 words instead of 15 ‚Äî do that.

üéØ

Always End With Next Steps

Never leave a customer hanging. Tell them exactly what happens next, when to expect it, and how to reach you if they need more help.
